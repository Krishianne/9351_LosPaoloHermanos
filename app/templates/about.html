Objectives

This study focuses on automating the classification of Arabica coffee bean grades using Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) and various data preprocessing techniques. It also compares the performance of CNN with traditional models like SVM and Decision Trees to assess improvements in accuracy and reliability. The final goal is to implement the most effective model into a web-based application for practical use in the coffee industry.

About

Grading coffee beans is essential in maintaining the quality, consistency, and market value of coffee, one of the most widely consumed beverages globally. The grading process evaluates beans based on various physical attributes such as size, flaws, moisture content, and cup quality, using methods like cupping, screening, and visual inspection (Cafe Du Monde, 2025).
